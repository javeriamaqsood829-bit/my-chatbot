FAQ

Common questions

Everything you might want to know before you start.

Is LinguaBridge really free to use?

Yes. There's no sign-up, subscription, or hidden limit — just open the page and start translating.

How accurate are the translations?

Translations are powered by the MyMemory Translation API, which draws on a large database of human and machine translations. It's great for everyday text, though nuanced or highly technical writing may need a human review.

Does the Website tab work on every site?

Not always. Some websites block automated requests for security reasons. If a site doesn't work, just copy its text and paste it into the Text tab instead.

Is my data stored anywhere?

No account or server-side storage is used. Your translation history and favorites live only in your browser for the current session.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes — the whole site is responsive and includes mobile-friendly features like camera capture for image translation and voice input.
